About Me

Akshat Jerath

Hello! I’m Akshat Jerath. I am an innovative and empathetic game designer currently studying the final year of BSc (Hons) Computer Games Design at Staffordshire University. I have a particular interest in creating levels for third person shooter and puzzle games and have experience developing for VR.

I have couple of hobbies but I like to travel and learn new things. I have been to few continents with my family . I love cars.
